Waste the Day

At night when I lie awake
I dream of all that I could have done today.
I could have sat and decided to bake a cake
Did I waste my day?
Did I do everything I had hoped of doing?
Did I push my tasks away?
Did I confront the feelings inside that were brewing?
Did I waste my day?
No, I did not waste my day!
Everyday is a time for celebration,
Do not worry about what others say.
I'll treat my days as a vacation.
So I never waste the day.
